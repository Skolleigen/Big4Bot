# Pricing & Packaging Strategy — References

## Foundational Works
- Thomas T. Nagle & Reed K. Holden: *The Strategy and Tactics of Pricing: A Guide to Growing More Profitably* (4th ed., 2010) — The definitive academic-practitioner textbook on pricing strategy; covers value-based pricing, price sensitivity measurement, competitive pricing dynamics, and pricing psychology
- Madhavan Ramanujam & Georg Tacke: *Monetizing Innovation: How Smart Companies Design the Product Around the Price* (2016) — Argues that pricing should be designed before the product, not after; introduces willingness-to-pay research methods and packaging design frameworks for SaaS and technology companies
- Hermann Simon: *Confessions of the Pricing Man: How Price Affects Everything* (2015) — Practitioner account from the founder of Simon-Kucher & Partners covering price elasticity, value communication, and the organizational challenges of pricing transformation

## Academic Sources
- Peter Westendorp: *NSS Price Sensitivity Meter*, ESOMAR Congress Proceedings (1976) — Original paper introducing the Van Westendorp Price Sensitivity Meter (PSM), the foundational willingness-to-pay research method used in pricing strategy engagements
- Eric J. Johnson & Daniel Goldstein: *Do Defaults Save Lives?*, Science (2003) — Behavioral economics research on choice architecture and defaults directly applicable to SaaS packaging design and plan presentation order
- Vineet Kumar: *Making Freemium Work*, Harvard Business Review (2014) — Empirical analysis of freemium conversion rates and feature gating strategies across 100+ software products; provides benchmarks for free-to-paid conversion optimization
- Barak Libai, Eitan Muller & Renana Peres: *The Diffusion of Services*, Journal of Marketing Research (2009) — Research on how pricing models affect adoption curves in subscription software markets

## Practitioner Resources
- Kyle Poyar (OpenView Partners): *SaaS Pricing and Packaging Benchmarks* (annual) — The leading practitioner benchmark for B2B SaaS pricing; covers tier structure, price point benchmarks, usage-based pricing adoption, NRR by pricing model, and packaging evolution trends
- Simon-Kucher & Partners: *Global Pricing Study* (annual) — Cross-industry practitioner research on pricing strategy maturity, price increase success rates, and value communication effectiveness; includes SaaS-specific findings
- Patrick Campbell (ProfitWell/Paddle): *The SaaS Pricing Page Teardown* series and *Pricing Strategy Guide* — Data-driven practitioner analysis of SaaS pricing page design, plan naming conventions, feature gating choices, and conversion optimization
- Tomasz Tunguz: *Winning with Data* (2016) and venture capital research on SaaS metrics — Widely cited practitioner writing on NRR benchmarks, expansion revenue mechanics, and the relationship between packaging structure and net dollar retention
