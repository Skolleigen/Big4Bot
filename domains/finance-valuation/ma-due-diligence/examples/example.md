# M&A Due Diligence: CloudTech Inc. Acquisition of DataStream LLC

## Input Context

**Problem Statement:** CloudTech Inc. has signed an LOI to acquire DataStream LLC for $150M ($120M base + $30M earn-out) based on an initial DCF valuation assuming 25% YoY revenue growth and $10M in year-1 cost synergies. We need to validate these assumptions through formal due diligence before signing the definitive agreement.

**Company Context:** CloudTech is an enterprise cloud storage provider. DataStream LLC is a high-growth data analytics startup with a proprietary AI querying engine.

**Strategic Deal Thesis:** CloudTech wants to acqui-hire DataStream's AI engineering team and cross-sell DataStream's analytics engine into CloudTech's existing enterprise storage customer base.

**Key Valuation Assumptions:** 
1. 25% standalone YoY revenue growth for DataStream over the next 3 years.
2. $10M in cost synergies by migrating DataStream's AWS hosting to CloudTech's bare-metal infrastructure.
3. $5M in Year-1 revenue synergies through cross-selling.

**Preliminary Target Information:** DataStream reports $20M ARR, 85% gross margins, and break-even EBITDA. The team consists of 50 engineers and 10 sales reps.
