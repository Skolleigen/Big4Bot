# Financial Ratio Analysis — References

## Foundational Works
- Eugene F. Brigham, Joel F. Houston: *Fundamentals of Financial Management* (2019, 15th ed.) — The leading undergraduate and practitioner reference for financial ratio analysis, covering liquidity, leverage, profitability, and efficiency ratios with industry benchmarking methodology.
- Robert C. Higgins: *Analysis for Financial Management* (2015, 11th ed.) — Practitioner-oriented financial analysis text widely used in MBA programs, with strong emphasis on ratio interpretation, trend analysis, and linking ratios to strategic decision-making.
- Aswath Damodaran: *Investment Valuation: Tools and Techniques for Determining the Value of Any Asset* (2012) — Includes comprehensive financial ratio analysis as a precursor to valuation, with guidance on adjusting reported ratios for operating leases, R&D capitalization, and nonrecurring items.

## Academic Sources
- CFA Institute: *CFA Program Curriculum, Level 1: Financial Reporting and Analysis* — The most comprehensive and widely adopted framework for financial ratio analysis used by investment professionals globally, covering all major ratio categories with cross-industry benchmarking.
- Edward I. Altman: *Financial Ratios, Discriminant Analysis and the Prediction of Corporate Bankruptcy*, Journal of Finance (1968) — Seminal academic paper introducing the Altman Z-Score, demonstrating how combinations of financial ratios predict financial distress, directly relevant to solvency ratio interpretation.
- James O. Horrigan: *A Short History of Financial Ratio Analysis*, The Accounting Review (1968) — Historical overview of financial ratio analysis from its origins in credit analysis to modern equity valuation applications.
- Richard J. Taffler: *Empirical Models for the Monitoring of UK Corporations*, Journal of Banking and Finance (1984) — UK counterpart to Altman's research, demonstrating the predictive power of liquidity and leverage ratios for financial distress identification.

## Practitioner Resources
- Risk Management Association (RMA): *Annual Statement Studies* — The industry-standard database of financial ratios by SIC code and revenue size, used by lenders and analysts as the benchmark source for industry-peer ratio comparison.
- Duff & Phelps (Kroll): *Valuation Handbook — Industry Cost of Capital* — Annual publication providing profitability and leverage ratio benchmarks by GICS industry sector, used in PE due diligence and credit analysis.
- S&P Global Market Intelligence: *Capital IQ Screening and Ratio Benchmarking Platform* — The primary institutional tool for extracting peer financial ratios and benchmarking targets against a customized comparable universe.
- Moody's Investors Service: *Financial Ratios and Metrics — A Guide to Corporate Credit Analysis* — Rating agency methodology document explaining how Moody's uses financial ratios (leverage, coverage, profitability, liquidity) to assign and monitor credit ratings.
