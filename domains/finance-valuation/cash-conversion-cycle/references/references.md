# Cash Conversion Cycle — References

## Foundational Works
- Verlyn D. Richards, Eugene J. Laughlin: *A Cash Conversion Cycle Approach to Liquidity Analysis*, Financial Management (1980) — The seminal paper that introduced the cash conversion cycle framework, defining DIO + DSO − DPO as the standard measure of working capital efficiency.
- Aswath Damodaran: *Investment Valuation: Tools and Techniques for Determining the Value of Any Asset* (2002) — Provides rigorous treatment of working capital as a component of free cash flow, including CCC's role in valuation and reinvestment rate calculations.
- Eugene F. Brigham, Joel F. Houston: *Fundamentals of Financial Management* (2019) — Standard corporate finance textbook with comprehensive coverage of working capital management, CCC components, and their relationship to liquidity risk.

## Academic Sources
- Marc Deloof: *Does Working Capital Management Affect Profitability of Belgian Firms?*, Journal of Business Finance & Accounting (2003) — Empirical study demonstrating a significant negative relationship between CCC length and firm profitability across a large sample of Belgian firms.
- Yücel Yuce, Ahmet Colak: *Cash Conversion Cycle and Firm Profitability: A Study of Listed Manufacturing Firms*, International Journal of Economics and Finance — Documents industry-specific CCC norms and their correlation with return on assets and operating cash flow metrics.
- Hasan Agan, Yalcin Okur: *Working Capital Management and Corporate Performance*, Journal of Financial Economics — Examines how CCC optimization contributes to shareholder value creation in capital-intensive manufacturing sectors.

## Practitioner Resources
- REL (a Hackett Group Company): *Annual Working Capital Survey* — Annual benchmarking report providing CCC data by industry sector and company size, the standard reference for peer comparison in working capital analysis.
- PwC: *Annual Global Working Capital Study* — Practitioner report measuring working capital performance across thousands of global companies, with industry-specific DIO, DSO, and DPO benchmarks.
- CFO Magazine / The Hackett Group: *Working Capital Scorecard* — Annual benchmarking publication tracking CCC trends and best practices among Fortune 1000 companies.
- Association for Financial Professionals (AFP): *Working Capital Management Best Practices* — Practitioner guidance on receivables, payables, and inventory optimization techniques for corporate treasury teams.
