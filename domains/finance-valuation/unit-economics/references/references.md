# Unit Economics — References

## Foundational Works
- David Skok: *SaaS Metrics 2.0 — A Guide to Measuring and Improving What Matters* (2012, For Entrepreneurs / SaaStr) — The definitive practitioner framework for SaaS unit economics, introducing and popularizing the LTV:CAC ratio, CAC payback period, and Magic Number as the standard metrics for evaluating SaaS business health.
- Bill Gurley: *All Revenue is Not Created Equal: The Keys to the 10X Revenue Club* (2011, Above the Crowd blog) — Landmark investor essay explaining how gross margin, churn, and net revenue retention compound to determine the quality and sustainability of SaaS revenue multiples.
- Andreessen Horowitz (a16z): *16 Metrics Every SaaS Founder Should Know* (2015) — Foundational a16z framework defining CAC, LTV, churn, and expansion revenue metrics with benchmarks used by institutional SaaS investors in Series A through growth equity rounds.

## Academic Sources
- Michael Braun, David A. Schweidel: *Modeling Customer Lifetimes with Multiple Causes of Churn*, Marketing Science (2011) — Academic treatment of customer lifetime value modeling under competing risk of churn, providing the statistical foundation for LTV calculations with heterogeneous customer populations.
- Peter Fader, Bruce Hardie: *How to Project Customer Retention*, Journal of Interactive Marketing (2007) — Foundational academic paper on probabilistic models of customer retention and CLV, directly applicable to SaaS churn rate modeling and LTV estimation.
- Sunil Gupta, Donald R. Lehmann: *Customers as Assets*, Journal of Interactive Marketing (2003) — Academic framework treating customer relationships as financial assets and introducing discounted cash flow approaches to LTV that parallel modern SaaS unit economics methodology.

## Practitioner Resources
- Bessemer Venture Partners: *State of the Cloud Report* (annual) — Annual benchmarking publication providing SaaS unit economics benchmarks (NRR, CAC payback, LTV:CAC) by company stage, ARR size, and GTM motion, widely used as the institutional standard for Series B/C readiness assessment.
- OpenView Partners: *SaaS Benchmarks Report* (annual) — Annual survey of SaaS company unit economics benchmarks by ARR range, providing CAC payback, NRR, and gross margin norms used to contextualize investor due diligence.
- KeyBanc Capital Markets: *Private SaaS Company Survey* (annual) — Annual benchmark report covering ARR growth rates, gross margins, CAC payback periods, and NRR across hundreds of private SaaS companies at various funding stages.
- Tomasz Tunguz (Redpoint Ventures): *SaaS Unit Economics and Benchmarks* (blog series, tomtunguz.com) — Data-driven practitioner analysis of SaaS unit economics trends, including CAC efficiency curves, churn benchmarks by market segment, and the empirical relationship between NRR and revenue multiples.
