# DuPont Analysis — References

## Foundational Works
- Donaldson Brown (DuPont Corporation): *Pricing Policy in Relation to Financial Control*, Management and Administration (1924) — The original internal DuPont memorandum introducing the ROE decomposition into margin, turnover, and leverage that forms the basis of all DuPont analysis frameworks.
- Stephen H. Penman: *Financial Statement Analysis and Security Valuation* (2013, 5th ed.) — The leading academic text on financial statement analysis, providing the most rigorous treatment of DuPont decomposition including the 5-factor model and its integration with equity valuation.
- Aswath Damodaran: *Investment Valuation: Tools and Techniques for Determining the Value of Any Asset* (2012) — Includes comprehensive discussion of DuPont analysis as a diagnostic tool for understanding the drivers of ROE and their implications for intrinsic value.

## Academic Sources
- Nissim Doron, Stephen H. Penman: *Financial Statement Analysis of Leverage and How It Informs About Profitability and Price-to-Book Ratios*, Journal of Accounting Research (2003) — Seminal academic paper extending the DuPont framework to separate operating and financial leverage effects, forming the basis of the modern 5-factor DuPont model.
- Robert Soliman: *The Use of DuPont Analysis by Market Participants*, The Accounting Review (2008) — Empirical study demonstrating that DuPont component changes predict future stock returns and earnings, validating the framework's analytical power for security valuation.
- Eugene F. Brigham, Joel F. Houston: *Fundamentals of Financial Management* (2019) — Standard treatment of DuPont decomposition as a diagnostic tool, including worked examples across manufacturing, retail, and financial services industries.

## Practitioner Resources
- McKinsey & Company: *Valuation: Measuring and Managing the Value of Companies* (Koller, Goedhart, Wessels, 2020) — Practitioner framework using DuPont-style ROIC decomposition (rather than ROE) as the primary operating performance diagnostic for enterprise value creation.
- CFA Institute: *CFA Program Curriculum, Level 1 and Level 2: Financial Reporting and Analysis* — Comprehensive pedagogical treatment of the 3-factor and 5-factor DuPont models with worked examples and peer benchmarking applications.
- Morningstar: *Financial Health and Profitability Analysis Methodology* — Documents how Morningstar equity analysts apply DuPont decomposition in systematic stock screening and fundamental research.
- Harvard Business School: *Note on Financial Ratio Analysis* (Technical Note 9-193-042) — HBS teaching note applying DuPont decomposition to case study companies, commonly used in MBA financial analysis courses.
