# Total Rewards Strategy: GrowthCo Tech Spin-off

## Input Context

**Problem Statement:** GrowthCo recently established a clear "Innovation and Agility" Employee Value Proposition (EVP) but is experiencing 25% annual turnover among critical software engineers. We need to restructure their rewards program to fix this retention issue without blowing the overall cash budget.

**Company Context:** A mid-market B2B software company. Historically a stable, slow-growth firm, they are now pivoting to an aggressive AI-focused product roadmap.

**Employee Value Proposition:** "Build the future of enterprise AI with unparalleled autonomy and aggressive upside for top performers."

**Current Rewards Spend:** $50M total compensation pool. Currently heavily weighted toward base salary (85%) with a standard 10% annual bonus and 5% standard 401(k) match for all employees.

**Employee Feedback/Demographics:** Pulse surveys indicate engineers feel under-compensated relative to big-tech competitors while non-technical staff value the high base salaries and retirement matching.
