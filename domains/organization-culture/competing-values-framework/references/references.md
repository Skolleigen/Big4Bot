# Competing Values Framework — References

## Foundational Works
- Robert Quinn & John Rohrbaugh: *A Spatial Model of Effectiveness Criteria: Towards a Competing Values Approach to Organizational Analysis*, Management Science (1983) — Original academic paper introducing the two-axis spatial model that became the theoretical foundation of the CVF; establishes the empirical basis for the four-quadrant culture typology
- Kim S. Cameron & Robert E. Quinn: *Diagnosing and Changing Organizational Culture: Based on the Competing Values Framework* (2011, 3rd ed.) — The definitive practitioner and academic reference for applying the CVF; includes the OCAI instrument, scoring methodology, and intervention guidance for each culture type

## Academic Sources
- Robert Quinn: *Beyond Rational Management: Mastering the Paradoxes and Competing Demands of High Performance* (1988) — Extends the CVF into leadership behavioral repertoires, linking each culture quadrant to distinct leadership roles and management competencies
- Kimberly Hartnell, Amy Yi Ou & Angelo Kinicki: *Organizational Culture and Organizational Effectiveness*, Journal of Applied Psychology (2011) — Meta-analysis of 84 studies examining the relationship between CVF culture types and performance outcomes including innovation, financial performance, and employee satisfaction

## Practitioner Resources
- OCAI Online: *OCAI Assessment Platform and Benchmarking Database* — Provides normative data across industries enabling comparison of organizational CVF scores against sector benchmarks; useful for contextualizing gap analysis findings
- Deloitte: *Core Beliefs and Culture* survey research — Practitioner research linking culture type to business performance outcomes and providing diagnostic frameworks for culture-strategy alignment assessments
- McKinsey & Company: *Organizational Culture in Mergers: Addressing the Unseen Forces* — Applied guidance for using culture type frameworks including CVF in M&A integration scenarios to assess cultural distance and design integration interventions
